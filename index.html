About Madam Tailor

For over 25 years, Madam Tailor has been the premier destination for discerning gentlemen seeking exceptional custom clothing. Our master tailors combine traditional Savile Row techniques with modern styling to create garments that are both timeless and contemporary.

We believe that every man deserves clothing that fits perfectly and reflects his personal style. From the finest fabrics to the smallest details, we ensure that every garment we create meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
